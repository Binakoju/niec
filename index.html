ABOUT US

Introduction

NIEC is a provider of short term exam preparation for exams like TOEFL, IELTS, SAT 1, GRE and GMAT. NIEC is also a study abroad consultancy that represents institutions around the world and places students in these institutions. NIEC is a language and computer institute. In addition, we are an authorized test center for the iBT and have been running trouble-free tests since 2006. NIEC is a part of a global network that focuses on the betterment of students and helps them to achieve their academic objectives.
